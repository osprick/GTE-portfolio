Mission

My mission is to bring education in life skills to anyone who needs them. The internet may jokingly call it "Adulting 101" but I do so seriously because so many people have a dearth of essential knowledge to survive in today's society. This includes managing personal finances, basic cooking skills, how to write a resume, effective job hunting, how to find a doctor, managing physical and mental health, and so much more. While much of this information can be found through internet serachs and much digging, I aim to build an online school that will be a central source of useful information in practical life skills. I believe knowledge opens doors to opportunity. My mission is to help people achieve self-actualization in life by giving them the effective tools and knowledge to do so.
